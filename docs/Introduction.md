# The Story

### Before I wrote code, I wanted to design cities.

<div style={{display: 'flex', gap: '2rem', alignItems: 'center', flexWrap: 'wrap-reverse', margin: '1rem 0'}}>
  <div style={{flex: '1', minWidth: '300px'}}>
    <p>Urban planning was the dream. I spent hours on city simulation games, imagining traffic flows and zoning regulations. Somewhere along the way, I discovered that software systems work the same way—roads become APIs, buildings become services, and zoning becomes architecture.</p>
    <p>That architect's eye never left me. It just found a different canvas.</p>
    <p>Now I build testing infrastructure for IoT devices, scale developer communities across continents, and create documentation systems that hundreds of engineers rely on. I've reviewed cheesecakes in 27 countries, collected enough conference badges to wallpaper a room, and still think in blueprints. <em><a href="/Multiverse">There are other versions of me that took different exits.</a></em></p>

### The North Star: Open Source

<div style={{display: 'flex', gap: '2rem', alignItems: 'center', flexWrap: 'wrap-reverse', margin: '1rem 0'}}>
  <div style={{flex: '0 0 auto'}}>
    <img src={require("./img/intro/talks.webp").default} style={{maxWidth: '450px', width: '100%', height: 'auto', borderRadius: '8px', boxShadow: '0 4px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.1)'}} alt="Speaking at conferences across the world" className="talks-image-desktop" />
  </div>
  <div style={{flex: '1', minWidth: '300px'}}>
    <p>In 2016, I fell in love with the idea of people building software together, for everyone, for free. Nine years later, that love hasn't faded—it's become a career.</p>
    <p>Open source isn't just code. It's the reason I've spoken at conferences across four continents, mentored students through Google Summer of Code, co-founded communities that grew to thousands of developers, and built infrastructure that tests 2000+ device releases every week.</p>
    <p>Everything I build, I document. Everything I learn, I share. That's not altruism—it's how the best systems scale. The architecture I dreamed of designing as a teenager? I'm building it now. Just with different materials.</p>
  </div>
</div>
